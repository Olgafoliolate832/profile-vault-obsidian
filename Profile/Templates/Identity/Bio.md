# Bio

> *Parent: [[Identity]]*

Bio variants at different lengths for different contexts. Maintain all three and update them together whenever your situation changes.

## How to Use

1. Write the **long** version first — it's the master narrative.
2. Derive **medium** and **short** by condensing, not rewriting from scratch.
3. Update all three together to keep them consistent.
4. Tailor for specific applications in the application's `Outputs/` folder, not here. These are your defaults.

# Identity

> *Parent: [[Templates]]*

Templates for your personal brand — who you are, how you present yourself, and your professional narrative. These are the "about me" building blocks that feed into CVs, applications, and public profiles.

## Structure

```
Identity/
├── Identity.md                  # Overview linking to all files
├── Personal-Info.md             # Name, contact, links, profiles
├── Bio.md                       # All bio variants (short, medium, long)
├── Research-Statement.md        # Research interests & vision
└── Professional-Summary.md      # Elevator pitch per domain
```

## Files

- [[Personal-Info]] — Name, contact details, online profiles, and links
- [[Bio]] — Bio variants at different lengths for different contexts
- [[Research-Statement]] — Research interests, vision, and trajectory
- [[Professional-Summary]] — Domain-specific elevator pitches

## Rules

1. **Keep content current** — update these files whenever your role, focus, or contact info changes.
2. **Personal-Info is the single source of truth** for your name, email, and links. Other files reference it.
3. **Bio variants live in one file** — short (~50 words), medium (~150 words), and long (~500 words) as sections.
4. **Professional Summary is domain-aware** — maintain separate pitches for academic, technical, and consulting contexts.
5. **Research Statement evolves** — update it when your research direction shifts. Keep previous versions in Archive.
6. **These files are pulled into applications** — Claude uses them when generating CVs, cover letters, and bios.
7. **Never delete old versions** — move superseded statements to `Archive/`.

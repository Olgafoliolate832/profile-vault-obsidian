# Grant Applications

> *Parent: [[Applications]]*

Templates for research funding proposals, grant applications, and project funding bids.

## Grant Applications

- [[Grant-N/Index|Grant-N]]

## Naming Convention

`YYYY-MM-Funder-ShortTitle` (e.g., `2026-04-FWF-AIMonitoring`, `2026-01-Horizon-ClimateData`)

## Typical Deliverables

- Grant profile / PI profile
- Budget summary (often linked to Excel)
- Project description / proposal narrative
- Work plan / Gantt chart
- Letters of support
- Ethics statement (if applicable)

## Type-Specific Guidance

- **Read the call carefully** — funders have specific evaluation criteria. Mirror their language in your proposal.
- **Track record matters** — link to your publications, past grants, and completed projects in the Mapping.
- **Budget alignment** — ensure requested amounts match the funder's range. Store detailed budgets in Excel under `Resources/`.
- **Consortium grants** (e.g., Horizon Europe): note partner roles and contributions in Research.md.
- **Resubmissions**: keep the previous version in Archive and note reviewer feedback in Strategy/Mapping.

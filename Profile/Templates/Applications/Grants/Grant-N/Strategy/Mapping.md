# Mapping

> *Parent: [[Strategy]]*

Map each evaluation criterion to your track record. This drives the narrative of your grant profile and proposal.

## Rules

- One row per evaluation criterion from [[Requirements]].
- **Strength**: `Strong`, `Medium`, or `Weak` — be honest.
- **Evidence**: link to specific notes in Grants-Funding, Publications, Projects, and Experience folders using wikilinks.
- For weak areas, identify collaborators or team members who fill the gap.
- The **Key Highlights** section drives your PI profile and proposal summary.
- **Proposed Angle** defines how you frame the research question to align with funder priorities.

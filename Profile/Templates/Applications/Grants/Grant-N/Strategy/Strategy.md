# Strategy

> *Parent: [[Index]]*

This folder contains **your analysis** — how your track record maps to the funder's criteria, where the gaps are, and what angle to propose. Complete this after filling in Context.

## How to Use

1. Read through all Context documents first.
2. Fill in [[Mapping]] — map each evaluation criterion to your track record from Grants-Funding, Publications, and Projects.
3. Identify gaps and plan mitigation (e.g., collaborators who fill gaps).
4. Define the proposal angle before drafting outputs.

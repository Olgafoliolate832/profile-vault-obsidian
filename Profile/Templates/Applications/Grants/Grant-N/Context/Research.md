# Research

> *Parent: [[Context]]*

Research the funder, their priorities, and past funded projects. Understanding what they value helps you frame your proposal strategically.

## Rules

- Check the funder's website for strategic plans, annual reports, and past awards.
- Look at previously funded projects — what topics, methods, and team sizes succeed?
- Note success rates if available — this informs how much effort to invest.
- Identify any connections (past reviewers, funded collaborators, program officers).

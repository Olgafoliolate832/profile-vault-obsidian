# Context

> *Parent: [[Index]]*

This folder contains all information **about the funder and the call** — what they fund, how they evaluate, and what they prioritize. This is input material that you gather before strategizing.

## How to Use

1. Start with [[Description]] — paste or summarize the funding call.
2. Extract eligibility and evaluation criteria in [[Requirements]].
3. Research the funder in [[Research]] — past funded projects, success rates, and strategic priorities.

## Context Documents

- [[Description]] — the funding call
- [[Requirements]] — eligibility, evaluation criteria, and required documents
- [[Research]] — funder priorities, past awards, and success tips

# Conference Submissions

> *Parent: [[Applications]]*

Templates for conference paper submissions, talk proposals, and workshop submissions.

## Conference Submissions

- [[Conference-N/Index|Conference-N]]

## Naming Convention

`YYYY-MM-Conference-Topic` (e.g., `2026-06-NeurIPS-GraphOpt`, `2026-09-CHI-UserStudy`)

## Typical Deliverables

- Abstract
- Full paper (if applicable)
- Speaker bio
- Presentation slides (post-acceptance)
- Supplementary materials

## Type-Specific Guidance

- **Match the track** — conferences have multiple tracks/themes. Pick the best fit and frame your work accordingly.
- **Know the format** — check word/page limits, citation style, and template requirements early.
- **Review criteria** — understand how submissions are evaluated. Address each criterion explicitly in your paper/abstract.
- **Resubmissions** — if resubmitting rejected work, note previous reviewer feedback in Strategy/Mapping and address each point.
- **Co-authors** — document co-author contributions and agreements in Index.md notes.
- **Deadlines are strict** — conference deadlines rarely extend. Track them carefully.

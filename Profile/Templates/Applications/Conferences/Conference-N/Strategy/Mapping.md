# Mapping

> *Parent: [[Strategy]]*

Map your work to the conference's topics and review criteria. This drives how you frame your abstract, paper, and presentation.

## Rules

- One row per topic or review criterion from [[Requirements]].
- **Strength**: `Strong`, `Medium`, or `Weak` — be honest about fit.
- **Evidence**: link to specific notes in Publications, Projects, and Research folders using wikilinks.
- Pick the **best-fitting track** — don't try to fit every topic.
- The **Key Highlights** drive your abstract and introduction.
- **Submission Angle** defines how you position your contribution relative to the conference's audience and themes.

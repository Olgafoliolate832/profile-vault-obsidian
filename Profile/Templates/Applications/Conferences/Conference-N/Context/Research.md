# Research

> *Parent: [[Context]]*

Research the conference, its audience, and past proceedings. This helps you frame your submission to match what the community values.

## Rules

- Check the conference's ranking and reputation in your field (CORE, ERA, h5-index).
- Browse past proceedings — what topics, methods, and styles get accepted?
- Note program committee members — their research interests influence what gets accepted.
- Identify any connections (committee members, past attendees, collaborators).
- Check acceptance rates if available — this informs effort investment.

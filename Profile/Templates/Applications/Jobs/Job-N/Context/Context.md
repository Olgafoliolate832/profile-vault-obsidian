# Context

> *Parent: [[Index]]*

This folder contains all information **about them** — the organization, the role, and what they are looking for. This is input material that you gather before strategizing.

## How to Use

1. Start with [[Description]] — paste or summarize the job posting.
2. Extract and organize requirements in [[Requirements]] — separate required vs. preferred qualifications.
3. Research the organization and team in [[Research]] — this informs your cover letter angle and interview preparation.

## Context Documents

- [[Description]] — the job posting
- [[Requirements]] — qualifications, skills, and other requirements
- [[Research]] — organization, team, culture, and recent developments

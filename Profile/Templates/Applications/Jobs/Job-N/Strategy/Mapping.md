# Mapping

> *Parent: [[Strategy]]*

Map each requirement to your evidence. This is the analytical core of your application — it drives what goes into your CV, cover letter, and other deliverables.

## Rules

- One row per requirement from [[Requirements]].
- **Strength**: `Strong`, `Medium`, or `Weak` — be honest.
- **Evidence**: link to specific notes in Experience, Skills, Publications, or Projects folders using wikilinks.
- Address every required qualification. Preferred qualifications are bonus.
- For weak areas, write a mitigation plan (transferable skills, willingness to learn, etc.).
- The **Key Highlights** section drives your cover letter — pick your 3 strongest matches.
- **Tone & Angle** sets the overall narrative for all deliverables.

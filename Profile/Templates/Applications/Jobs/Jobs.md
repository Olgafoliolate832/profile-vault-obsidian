# Job Applications

> *Parent: [[Applications]]*

Templates for job and position applications across academic, industry, and consulting roles.

## Job Applications

- [[Job-N/Index|Job-N]]

## Naming Convention

`YYYY-MM-Organization-Role` (e.g., `2026-03-Google-SeniorMLE`, `2026-04-TU-Wien-Postdoc`)

## Typical Deliverables

- Tailored CV / Résumé
- Cover Letter
- Research Statement (academic roles)
- Teaching Statement (academic roles)
- Portfolio or work samples (if requested)

## Type-Specific Guidance

- **Academic roles**: emphasize publications, grants, teaching, and service. Use the academic CV template from `Templates/`.
- **Industry roles**: emphasize projects, technical skills, and measurable impact. Keep CV to 1-2 pages.
- **Consulting roles**: emphasize client work, strategy, and business outcomes.
- **Always check** if the role requires domain-specific documents beyond CV and cover letter.
- **Tailor every CV** — never submit a generic one. The Strategy/Mapping step ensures this.

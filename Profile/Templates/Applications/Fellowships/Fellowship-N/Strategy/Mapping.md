# Mapping

> *Parent: [[Strategy]]*

Map each selection criterion to your profile. This drives your research statement, cover letter, and overall narrative.

## Rules

- One row per selection criterion from [[Requirements]].
- **Strength**: `Strong`, `Medium`, or `Weak` — be honest.
- **Evidence**: link to specific notes in Experience, Publications, Projects, and Skills folders using wikilinks.
- For weak areas, explain how the fellowship itself addresses the gap (that's the point of a fellowship).
- The **Key Highlights** drive your cover letter and research statement.
- **Research Angle** connects your past, the host institution, and your future vision into a coherent story.

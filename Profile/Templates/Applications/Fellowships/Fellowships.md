# Fellowship Applications

> *Parent: [[Applications]]*

Templates for fellowship, scholarship, and research mobility applications.

## Fellowship Applications

- [[Fellowship-N/Index|Fellowship-N]]

## Naming Convention

`YYYY-MM-Organization-Program` (e.g., `2026-05-Humboldt-PostdocFellowship`, `2026-03-Fulbright-Scholar`)

## Typical Deliverables

- CV / Résumé
- Research Statement / Research Plan
- Cover Letter / Motivation Letter
- Reference Letters (coordinated via References folder)
- Host institution confirmation (if required)

## Type-Specific Guidance

- **Research vision is key** — fellowships value independence and a clear research trajectory.
- **Host institution fit** — explain why the specific institution and supervisor/group are the right match.
- **Career narrative** — connect past experience, current interests, and future plans into a coherent story.
- **Mobility component** — many fellowships (Humboldt, Marie Curie, Fulbright) require international mobility. Address this explicitly.
- **Timeline awareness** — fellowship cycles are often annual with long lead times. Start early.

# PhD Applications

> *Parent: [[Applications]]*

Templates for doctoral program and PhD position applications.

## PhD Applications

- [[PhD-N/Index|PhD-N]]

## Naming Convention

`YYYY-MM-University-Topic` (e.g., `2026-06-ETH-MachineLearning`, `2026-04-UniVienna-DigitalHumanities`)

## Typical Deliverables

- CV / Résumé
- Research Proposal
- Cover Letter / Motivation Letter
- Writing sample (if requested)
- Transcripts
- Reference Letters (coordinated via References folder)

## Type-Specific Guidance

- **Research proposal** is usually the most important document. Show that you understand the field and have a feasible plan.
- **Supervisor alignment** — research the supervisor's work thoroughly. Reference their publications in your proposal.
- **Funding awareness** — note whether the position is funded, requires separate funding applications, or is self-funded.
- **Language requirements** — check and document language proficiency requirements early.
- **Structured vs. individual** — structured PhD programs have different requirements than individual doctoral positions. Note this in Requirements.md.

# Context

> *Parent: [[Index]]*

This folder contains all information **about the PhD program or position** — the university, the supervisor, and what they require. This is input material that you gather before strategizing.

## How to Use

1. Start with [[Description]] — paste or summarize the PhD position or program details.
2. Extract academic and research requirements in [[Requirements]].
3. Research the supervisor and department in [[Research]] — their publications, research group, and strategic fit.

## Context Documents

- [[Description]] — the PhD program or position
- [[Requirements]] — academic, research, document, and language requirements
- [[Research]] — university, supervisor, research group, and strategic fit

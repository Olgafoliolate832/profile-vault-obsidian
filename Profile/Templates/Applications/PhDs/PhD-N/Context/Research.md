# Research

> *Parent: [[Context]]*

Research the university, department, supervisor, and research group. This is critical for writing a compelling research proposal and motivation letter.

## Rules

- Read the supervisor's recent publications — reference them in your proposal.
- Check the research group's website — who are the current members, what are they working on?
- Note the department's strengths and how your interests align.
- Look for any personal connections or shared research interests.
- Check university rankings and reputation in your specific field.

# Mapping

> *Parent: [[Strategy]]*

Map each requirement to your profile. This drives your research proposal, motivation letter, and CV tailoring.

## Rules

- One row per requirement from [[Requirements]].
- **Strength**: `Strong`, `Medium`, or `Weak` — be honest.
- **Evidence**: link to specific notes in Education, Experience, Publications, Skills, and Projects folders using wikilinks.
- For weak areas, explain how the PhD itself addresses the gap (learning new methods, gaining experience).
- The **Key Highlights** drive your motivation letter — pick your 3 strongest matches.
- **Research Proposal Angle** connects your background to the supervisor's work and your proposed contribution.

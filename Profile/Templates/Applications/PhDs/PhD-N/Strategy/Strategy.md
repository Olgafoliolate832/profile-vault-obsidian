# Strategy

> *Parent: [[Index]]*

This folder contains **your analysis** — how your profile matches their requirements, where the gaps are, and what research proposal angle to take. Complete this after filling in Context.

## How to Use

1. Read through all Context documents first.
2. Fill in [[Mapping]] — map each requirement to your evidence from Education, Experience, Publications, and Skills.
3. Identify gaps and plan how to address them.
4. Define your research proposal angle, connecting it to the supervisor's work.
